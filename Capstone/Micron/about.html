About Micron

    Micron been selling and servicing business equipment for over 35 years. We provide printer repair and maintenance for all HP, OKI, Lexmark and Canon printers and fax machines. Our technicians specialize in rapid on site service and can typically make same day calls. Our Preventative Maintenance plan and low cost service agreements can save you a great deal of printer down time and expense. We also make local toner deliveries and pick

    At Micron, we do not have a Customer Service Department. It just didn’t seem fair to ask a group of our team to be accountable for the actions of the rest of the team. Every member of Micron is a customer service agent. We work hard to repair your equipment quickly and effectively, usually in one visit. If you every have to call for further assistance we promise you will be treated like our only client until the matter is resolved.

History

    Harold Palmer is the founder of Micron Business Products. MBP got its start in 1972, and its employees took pride in providing the best in office machines and repairs. MBP began rebuilding toner cartridges in 1992, and a manager was hired who was specifically trained in laser technology.

    The world of business—and its technology—has changed completely since 1972. Many companies in the Madison area were offering similar customer goods and care then, but Micron is the only one of those companies still in existence today. Harold Palmer, Founder     A transplant from West Virginia, Harold was a salesman for a candy maker in Chicago when he became interested in business machines. As he moved from sales rep to district manager, Harold made many of the personal contacts that allowed him to start Micron Business Products in 1972. Micron originally sold calculators and other business machines, and its original store was a former funeral home.
